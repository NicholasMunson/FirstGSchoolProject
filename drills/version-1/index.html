  • Today went okay. I hit many of my goals but not all of them. fell short on that goal and plan to make it top priority tomorrow. I did however, finish my kickbacks. I was feeling a bit defeated, but I used the developer tools in Chrome to figure out my mistakes. I completed this task around 2:30. The time I was planning on starting B had already passed so I decided to draw up a whiteboard plan for B and finish my kickbacks by stand-down (4:30pm). I took too much time on my goals for A so that is why this goal was not met. My kickbacks went well because I knew what I did wrong and how to fix them. After using command line for a few weeks,I am much faster with it and I have a much better understanding then when I originally did the task. I am feeling confident that they will pass now.

  • Questions

  • What could I have done to make A go smoother?

    I believe that I would not have had this problem if I would have planned out my HTML structure more in the whiteboard stage. In the future I will take more time in the whiteboard stage to ensure that my plans will work. This will lower the amount of time I will need to trouble shoot.

    I think if I started with my kickbacks I would have had more momentum going into my goals and would have felt less defeated and been in a better mindset.
